About me

Hello! I'm Tilani Ikeshala Peiris, an IT graduate from the University of Moratuwa (2020) with a passion for UI/UX design. Although I'm relatively new to the field, my journey involves diving into the world of UI/UX through practical experiences and continuous learning.

Design Philosophy

My design journey revolves around crafting intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. Leveraging practical knowledge in Figma, I engage in creating case studies and prototypes that showcase my evolving skills. My proficiency extends to utilizing tools like Balsamiq Wireframes and occasionally Wireframe.cc for varied design perspectives.

Design Approach

My focus lies in creating impactful UIs through meticulous case studies and Figma prototypes. I am not just a designer; I'm on a journey to evolve into a UI/UX Engineer, bridging the gap between aesthetics and functionality.
