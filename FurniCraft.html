FurniCraft - Furniture Shop Mobile App

Developed and designed an engaging mobile app, "FurniCraft," catering to a seamless furniture shopping experience. The app encompasses several key features, enhancing user interaction and satisfaction:

  • Home Section: Crafted an inviting and user-friendly home screen that intuitively guides users to explore featured items and promotions.
  • Items Under Categories: Implemented a well-organized categorization system, allowing users to effortlessly browse furniture items based on their preferences and needs.
  • Product Details & Cart Functionality: Designed an intuitive interface for users to view detailed information about selected items, add them to the cart, and manage the shopping cart effortlessly.
  • Checkout Process: Streamlined the checkout process, ensuring a smooth transition from cart to checkout. Users can review their selected items, and complete the purchase securely.
  • User Profile: Developed a personalized user profile section, enabling users to track order history, manage preferences, and seamlessly navigate through the app.
