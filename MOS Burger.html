MOS Burger Shop Management System - Web Application

Designed and developed a comprehensive web application for "MOS Burgers," a local medium-scale business specializing in burgers, submarines, and soft drinks. The application addresses the challenges posed by manual transaction processes, aiming to streamline and modernize operations.

Home Section

  • Displays all food items categorically, including Burgers, Submarines, Fries, Pasta, Chicken, and Beverages.
  • Each food item is presented with its price, quantity on hand, expiration dates for packaged foods, and a unique food item code.

Search Section

  • Enables users to search for specific food items using names, item codes, or categories.

Store Management

  • Allows users to add new items to the system.
  • Provides capabilities to update and delete existing food items.
  • Notifies users about expired food items and facilitates their removal.

Order Management

  • Facilitates order placement and tracking of order details.
  • Allows users to select food items by name, item code, or category.
  • Displays essential information such as price, expiration warning, item code, and applicable discounts.
  • Cart functionality for displaying selected food items with prices and quantities.
  • Discount feature for the entire order.
  • View and manage previous orders, with the ability to edit, delete, and update order details.

Customer Management

  • Stores customer details for future campaigns and offers.
  • Enables the addition of customers during order placement or through a separate process.
  • Allows updating, deleting, and editing customer details.
  • Provides a view of orders under each customer's name.

Report Generating

  • Monthly sales report generation.
  • Identification of customers with the highest rate of orders within the month.
  • Annual sales report and food items count report generation.
